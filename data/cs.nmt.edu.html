About the Department

New Mexico Tech, formally known as New Mexico Institute of Mining and Technology, is an educational and research institute specializing in science, engineering, and technology. It has been consistently highly ranked by publications such as Princeton Review, Kiplinger’s, and U.S. News and World Report.

Established in 1966, the Department of Computer Science at New Mexico Tech offers courses in both theoretical and applied computer science covering current concepts and major applications of the field as part of the following programs:

  • Ph.D. in Computer Science
  • Master’s in Computer Science
  • Bachelor’s in Computer Science
  • Bachelor’s in Information Technology
  • Minor in Computer Science

Since 2007, the department has been accredited by the Computing Accreditation Commission of ABET Inc. The program’s educational objectives as well as information about our ABET Accreditation can be found on our Bachelor of Science in Computer Science page.

For information about Institute-wide accreditation, please visit the NMT accreditation page.

Our graduate programs provide students the opportunity to take courses, select advisory committee members, and pursue research in an area of interest to a faculty supervisor. The Master of Science program is designed for students who wish to further broaden or deepen their knowledge of computer science; both thesis and non-thesis options are offered. The Ph. D. program is appropriate for students with motivation for research and either a superior track record in coursework or substantial experience in industrial research and development.

In addition to teaching assistantships and departmental research assistantships, many computer science graduate students receive support from New Mexico Tech’s various research divisions such as ICASA, PRRC, and EMRTC.

In 2002, New Mexico Tech received designation as a Center of Academic Excellence in Information Assurance Education from the National Security Agency.  The department has not only renewed its NSA/DHS National Center of Academic Excellence in Information Assurance Education status, but also added the prestigious designation of NSA/DHS National Center of Academic Excellence in Information Assurance Research.
